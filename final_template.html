Unionization in the Private Sector is on the Decline, but Efforts to Unionize are not

Unionization efforts in private companies have recently made national headlines. With unionization in the private sector on the decline, these efforts show a spark that does not seem to be burning out any time soon. The more corporations try to shut down unionization efforts, the more oxygen they blow on the fire that they started. In fact, the fire started in Buffalo by Starbucks employees has already begun spreading to Arizona. The votes will be counted in a matter of days. The results of the vote could set a precedent that would light up corporate America.
